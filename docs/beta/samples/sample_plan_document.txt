SUMMARY OF BENEFITS AND COVERAGE
Acme Manufacturing Health Plan — PPO Option
Coverage Period: 01/01/2026 — 12/31/2026
Coverage For: Employee + Family | Plan Type: PPO

This is only a summary. If you want more detail about your coverage and costs,
you can get the complete terms in the policy or plan document at acmehealth.example.com
or by calling 1-800-555-0188.


IMPORTANT QUESTIONS

What is the overall deductible?
  In-Network: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
  Out-of-Network: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family

Are there services covered before you meet your deductible?
  Yes. Preventive care and prescription drugs are covered before you meet your deductible.

Are there other deductibles for specific services?
  No. There is one combined medical and pharmacy deductible.

What is the out-of-pocket limit for this plan?
  In-Network: $5,000 individual / $10,000 family
  Out-of-Network: $10,000 individual / $20,000 family

What is not included in the out-of-pocket limit?
  Premiums, balance-billing charges, and health care this plan doesn't cover.

Will you pay less if you use a network provider?
  Yes. See acmehealth.example.com for a list of network providers.

Do you need a referral to see a specialist?
  No.


COMMON MEDICAL EVENTS — WHAT YOU WILL PAY

Primary care visit to treat an injury or illness
  In-Network: $25 copay/visit, deductible does not apply
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance

Specialist visit
  In-Network: $50 copay/visit, deductible does not apply
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance

Preventive care / screening / immunization
  In-Network: No charge
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance

Diagnostic test (x-ray, blood work)
  In-Network: $30 copay
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance

Imaging (CT/PET scans, MRIs)
  In-Network: $200 copay
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance


PRESCRIPTION DRUG COVERAGE — 4-TIER FORMULARY

Tier 1 — Generic drugs
  Retail (30-day): $10 copay
  Mail-order (90-day): $20 copay

Tier 2 — Preferred brand drugs
  Retail (30-day): $35 copay
  Mail-order (90-day): $70 copay

Tier 3 — Non-preferred brand drugs
  Retail (30-day): $70 copay
  Mail-order (90-day): $140 copay

Tier 4 — Specialty drugs
  20% coinsurance up to $250 maximum per prescription
  Specialty drugs must be filled through the Plan's designated specialty pharmacy.
  Mail-order is not available for specialty drugs.

Prescription drug benefit administered by: National PBM Services, Inc.
Formulary subject to change with 60 days notice.


HOSPITAL & SURGICAL SERVICES

Inpatient hospital stay (facility fee)
  In-Network: $500 copay/admission, then 20% coinsurance after deductible
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance after deductible

Inpatient physician/surgeon fees
  In-Network: 20% coinsurance after deductible
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance after deductible

Outpatient surgery (facility fee)
  In-Network: $250 copay, then 20% coinsurance after deductible
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance after deductible

Emergency room services
  In-Network: $300 copay, then 20% coinsurance (copay waived if admitted)
  Out-of-Network: $300 copay, then 20% coinsurance (paid as in-network for true emergencies)

Urgent care
  In-Network: $50 copay
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance


MENTAL HEALTH, BEHAVIORAL HEALTH, OR SUBSTANCE ABUSE NEEDS

Outpatient services
  In-Network: $25 copay/visit
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance

Inpatient services
  In-Network: $500 copay/admission, then 20% coinsurance after deductible
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance after deductible


PREGNANCY

Office visits
  In-Network: No charge
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance

Childbirth/delivery professional services
  In-Network: 20% coinsurance after deductible
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance after deductible

Childbirth/delivery facility services
  In-Network: $500 copay/admission, then 20% coinsurance after deductible
  Out-of-Network: 40% coinsurance after deductible


EXCLUDED SERVICES & OTHER COVERED SERVICES

Services this plan does NOT cover (this is not a complete list):
  - Cosmetic surgery
  - Long-term care
  - Routine eye care for adults
  - Routine foot care
  - Weight loss programs

Other covered services (this is not a complete list — limitations may apply):
  - Acupuncture (limited to 12 visits/year)
  - Chiropractic care (limited to 20 visits/year)
  - Hearing aids (one device per ear every 36 months)
  - Infertility treatment
  - Non-emergency care when traveling outside the U.S.
